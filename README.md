🚀 IdeaLens AI

IdeaLens AI is an AI-powered platform that transforms raw startup ideas into structured insights, market analysis, and actionable plans.

🌐 Live App: https://idea-lens-ai.vercel.app/

💡 What It Does

IdeaLens AI takes a simple idea like:

"AI resume builder"

…and turns it into a detailed analysis including:

📊 Idea Score

📈 Market Size

⚔️ Competition Analysis

💰 Monetization Strategies

🧠 AI-Powered Suggestions

✨ Features
🧠 AI Idea Analysis

Converts raw ideas into structured business insights

Provides a clear idea score (e.g., 7/10)

Explains strengths and weaknesses

📊 Market Research

Estimates market size and growth

Highlights industry trends

Provides data-backed insights

⚔️ Competitor Breakdown

Identifies key competitors

Explains competition level

Shows differentiation opportunities
